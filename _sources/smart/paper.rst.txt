Paper and citation
==================

Advancing MAPF Toward the Real World: A Scalable Multi-Agent Realistic
Testbed (SMART)

Summary
-------

The SMART testbed provides a realistic environment for evaluating Multi-Agent
Path Finding (MAPF) algorithms. It couples physics-engine based simulation to
better model kinodynamics and execution uncertainty, uses an Action Dependency
Graph for execution monitoring, and focuses on scaling to large numbers of
agents.
